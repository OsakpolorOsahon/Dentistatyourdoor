‎Dentist At Your Door – Full Business Profile
‎
‎Business Overview & Founder
‎
‎Name: Dentist At Your Door (also styled “Dentist@yourdoor” or “Dentistatyourdoor”)
‎
‎Founder: Dr. Yemisi Akintonwa, BDS
‎
‎Founded: 2017
‎
‎Concept: A mobile dental clinic in Lagos, Nigeria, providing dental care directly at clients’ homes or offices to reduce missed appointments due to traffic, anxiety, or time constraints. The business also operates physical branches in Lekki and Festac Town.
‎
‎Services Offered
‎Dentist At Your Door provides a wide range of dental care services including:
‎
‎Preventive Care: Scaling, polishing, deep cleaning
‎
‎Restorative Dentistry: Fillings, root canals, crowns, bridges, dentures, implants
‎
‎Cosmetic Dentistry: Teeth whitening, “Hollywood smiles”, veneers, aligners
‎
‎Orthodontics: Invisible aligners and alignment correction
‎
‎Maxillofacial Surgery: Surgical extractions, jaw-related procedures
‎
‎Pediatric Dentistry: Child-friendly dental care using "Tell-Show-Do" techniques
‎
‎Virtual Consultations: Remote consultations and treatment planning
